# Epic 07: Impression & Analytics

## Overview

This epic covers all impression-related functionality including recording impressions, verification, proof-of-play, quality scoring, duplicate detection, and dispute handling. Impressions are the core billing unit that tracks when ads are actually displayed to viewers.

## Business Value

- Provide accurate billing based on verified impressions
- Build trust through transparent proof-of-play
- Enable quality-based pricing adjustments
- Handle disputes fairly and efficiently

## Goals

- Record all impressions accurately and reliably
- Verify impression validity and quality
- Provide proof-of-play for advertisers
- Handle disputes and discrepancies

## Technical Notes

### Impression Quality Factors
| Factor | Weight | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Duration | 30% | Full play vs partial |
| Visibility | 25% | Screen fully visible |
| Device Health | 20% | Device operating normally |
| Time Accuracy | 15% | Within scheduled window |
| Uniqueness | 10% | Not a duplicate |
