# Epic 06: Content Management

## Overview

This epic covers all content-related functionality including content types and specifications, lifecycle management, upload and processing, content moderation, library management, and content delivery. Content consists of the advertising assets (images, videos, HTML5) that are displayed on devices.

## Business Value

- Enable advertisers to manage their creative assets
- Ensure content quality through automated and manual moderation
- Support multiple content formats and specifications
- Provide efficient content delivery to devices

## Goals

- Support multiple content types and formats
- Implement complete content lifecycle
- Enable automated and manual content moderation
- Build efficient content library management
