# Epic 03: Supplier & Store Management

## Overview

This epic covers all supplier (retail partner) functionality including supplier account management, store onboarding, device deployment, competitor blocking, and performance metrics. Suppliers are retail businesses that own and operate physical stores where advertising devices are deployed.

## Business Value

- Onboard retail partners efficiently
- Enable detailed store-level management
- Protect supplier relationships through competitor blocking
- Track and reward supplier performance

## Goals

- Implement complete supplier account lifecycle
- Enable store management with detailed configurations
- Implement competitor blocking for advertiser conflicts
- Support performance tracking and tiered benefits

- Store configuration accuracy > 95%
